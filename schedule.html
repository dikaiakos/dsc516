Module 1: Introduction to Cloud Computing, Fundamental Concepts and Models
Lecture 1: Introduction. Notes

  • Be familiar with the concepts of Middleware, Edge, Cloud, IAAS, PAAS, SAAS, Fog, Microservices.
Readings:
  • EPL699 course syllabus and contract.
Videos:

Precept 1: 4/9/2019 Teams, Tools and Resources. Notes
Learning outcome:
  • Be familiar with online resources for entrepreneurship, start-ups and innovation.
  • Learn some basic collaboration, communication, and ideation tools: Trello, Slack, SimpleMind.
  • Understand the concepts of agile knowledge work with Scrum.
  • Start forming your teams of 3-4 people and brainstorm on the idea each team will work on.
  • Understand the value of mentorship and seek a mentor.
  • Familiarity with the C4E Makerspace.
Readings:
Team formation and roles
  • Finding a Co-founder in College by Aaron Harris, Y Combinator.
  • Paths in the Tech Industry: Founder, Executive, or Employee by Michael Seibel, Y Combinator.
  • Technical co-founder by Adora Cheung, Y Combinator.
Tools for Ideation, Coordination, Prototyping and Execution
  • A View of Cloud Computing by Michael Armbrust, Armando Fox, Rean Griffith, Anthony D. Joseph, Randy Katz, Andy Konwinski, Gunho Lee, David Patterson, Ariel Rabkin, Ion Stoica, Matei Zaharia Communications of the ACM, April 2010, Vol. 53 No. 4, Pages 50-58.
  • What are the pros and cons of the waterfall and agile/scrum project management approach? Quora.
  • Review the Trello platform for team management.
  • Review the Slack platform for group communication.
  • Review SimpleMind for ideation.
  • Review Heroku, a Cloud Application platform.
  • Review Scrum and agile.
  • Rapid App Prototyping with Proto.io.
  • Review capabilities of the C4E Makerspace.
  • Review the capabilities of the Open Innovation Platform by the Netkite project.
Videos:
  • How to Find a Cofounder by Kat Manalac, Y Combinator.
  • Technical co-founder by Adora Cheung, Y Combinator.
  • How to Work Together by Kevin Hale, Y Combinator.
  • Building applications with Heroku.
